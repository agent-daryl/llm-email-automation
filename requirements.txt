llm-email-automation
================
Python 3.8+

No external dependencies (uses standard library only:

- smtplib (SMTP operations)
- imaplib (IMAP operations)
- email (RFC822 message parsing)
- argparse (CLI interface)
- email.header (MIME header decoding)
- os (filesystem operations)
- sys (system operations)
- typing (type hints)
- re (regex for email validation)
- pathlib (path operations)
- glob (file globbing)
